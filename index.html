About Luke's Quiz Game

This quiz was created with the goal of testing the knowledge of IB Computer Science students. The questions cover a range of topics that are relevant to the IB curriculum, aiming to help students prepare for exams by offering an interactive and challenging quiz format.

The reason for creating this quiz is to provide a resource for students who are looking to test their understanding of key concepts in Computer Science and help them assess their readiness for the IB exam.
